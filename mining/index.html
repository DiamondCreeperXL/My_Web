Mining

About Mining

Mining is a well known activity in Minecraft because mine is in the name Minecraft. The number #1 rule in Minecraft is to never dig down. Also make sure you bring enough torches for light. If you don't have coal to make torches then make charcoal by burning wood in a furnace and then use anything wood to fuel the furance. After you get the charcoal, make a crafting table and get some wood so you can turn it into wooden planks then into sticks. After that, make torches by putting them in the crafting table with the coal orcharcoal on the top and the sticks on the bottom. Make sure when you mine you have a weapon just in case a hostile mob tries to kill you. Also bring some food during the journey so you won't die. Also if you don't know gold, redstone, lapis lazuli, and diamond ore can only be mined with an iron pickaxe.
