Fighting

Fighting is another avtivity important in Minecraft because in Minecraft there are mobs that can hurt you and mobs that drop food when you kill them. Some mobs that drop food are pigs,cows,bunnies or rabbits,and sheep. It is very important to keep your hunger bar full and just in case if something attacks you can run away and hide and wait untill your hearts refill if your hunger bar is full. Also if your hunger bar is empty you will die from hunger.
